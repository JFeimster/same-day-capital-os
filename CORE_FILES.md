# CORE SYSTEM FILES (RECOMMENDED)

This document outlines the remaining high-impact markdown files to build
for Same-Day Capital OS.

These files expand intelligence, improve conversions, and create a complete
funding operating system.

---

## 💰 FUNDING INTELLIGENCE

### 1. UNDERWRITING.md
Defines approval logic and risk evaluation.

Include:
- Revenue thresholds
- Time in business tiers
- NSF / negative day limits
- Credit considerations
- Approval likelihood tiers (High / Medium / Low)
- Common decline reasons

---

### 2. FUNDING_PROGRAMS.md
Breakdown of all funding options.

Include:
- Small ticket (same-day funding)
- Mid-tier funding
- Large funding
- Line of credit
- Who each program is best for
- Typical ranges + expectations

---

### 3. REPAYMENT_MODELS.md
Explains how repayment works.

Include:
- Daily vs weekly payments
- Factor rates explained simply
- Early payoff benefits
- Example repayment scenarios

---

## 💬 SALES & CONVERSION

### 4. OBJECTIONS.md
Central objection handling system.

Include:
- “Rates are too high”
- “I’ll think about it”
- “I don’t need funding”
- “I’ll go to a bank”
- Trust concerns

Structure:
- Objection
- Reframe
- Simplified explanation
- Close/CTA

---

### 5. CTA_ROUTER.md
Controls how and when CTAs are deployed.

Include:
- Intent → CTA mapping
- Curious vs ready vs hesitant flows
- Rules for when to push vs hold back
- CTA phrasing variations

---

### 6. SCRIPTS.md
Centralized outreach and follow-up scripts.

Include:
- Cold outreach
- Warm outreach
- Referral scripts
- Follow-up sequences
- Re-engagement messages

---

## 📊 DATA & ANALYSIS

### 8. DEAL_EXAMPLES.md
Real-world funding scenarios.

- Revenue → funding outcomes

- Industry-specific examples
- Before/after funding scenarios
